* Software for project design and management

June 15, 2018

When I cooperate with others developers and designers I realize there is no available open-source sytem for small teams that should support their cooperations. Application is deployed on Heroku server and files are stored in AWS S3. In this phase of development I am designing new appereance and designing important parts of the system. Great advantage of Django framework is already implemented interface for admins.

When I was in High School I have learned many useful skills. Designing in AutoCAD was my favourite part of schooling lectures and I obtain a lot of knowledge about enginnering components, dimensions and materials. Because of this, I decided to make heli- copter rotor as my final work which was important part of my graduation. This work is exact copy of RC helicopter with series number 18H-M0629.

When I look back it gave me a lot of experience about 3D modeling and engineering structures projections.
